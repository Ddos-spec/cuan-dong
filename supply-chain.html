Supply Chain & Logistics

From farm to container — our end-to-end export process explained.

Export Process

How We Move Your Cargo

Every shipment follows a rigorous process from procurement to final delivery. We handle all logistics coordination so you don't have to.

Step 1

RFQ & Contract

Proforma invoice issued within 48h. Sales contract signed. Payment term agreed (T/T, L/C, or D/P).

Step 2

Production & Procurement

Direct sourcing from partner farms/cooperatives. Grading, sorting, and processing to spec.

Step 3

Quality Control Inspection

In-house QC + optional third-party inspection (SGS / Sucofindo). CoA issued before loading.

Step 4

Packaging & Stuffing

Custom packaging per buyer spec. Container stuffing supervised at approved warehouse.

Step 5

Documentation & Compliance

Commercial invoice, packing list, B/L, Phytosanitary certificate, CoO, halal cert — all prepared.

Step 6

Shipment & Tracking

Cargo loaded from Tanjung Priok or Tanjung Perak. Tracking info shared with buyer in real-time.

Trade Terms

Incoterms We Offer

We're flexible with trade terms. Choose what works best for your supply chain.

FOB
Most Popular
Free on Board
We deliver to the named port and load onto the vessel. Risk transfers to buyer once aboard. Buyer arranges freight & insurance. Best for experienced importers.
CIF
Cost, Insurance & Freight
We handle cost of goods, marine insurance, and freight to the destination port. Risk transfers at loading port. Ideal for first-time importers or buyers who prefer simplicity.
EXW
Ex Works
Buyer collects from our warehouse. Maximum buyer control over logistics. Best for buyers with freight forwarders in Indonesia. Lowest risk for us, highest for buyer.
Export Documents

What's Included in Every Shipment

📄

Commercial Invoice

Full itemized invoice with HS codes, unit price, and total value.

📦

Packing List

Detailed breakdown of cartons, weights, and container seal numbers.

🌿

Phytosanitary Cert

Issued by Indonesian Ministry of Agriculture. Required for all plant-based exports.

🚢

Bill of Lading

Original B/L issued by shipping line. Full set (3 originals) provided.

🏅

Certificate of Origin

Form D (ASEAN-FTA) or general CoO from KADIN Indonesia.

🔬

Certificate of Analysis

Lab test results: moisture, aflatoxin, pesticide residue, microbiology.

☪️

Halal Certificate

MUI-issued Halal certification. Critical for Middle East and SE Asia markets.

🛡

Insurance Certificate

Marine cargo insurance (CIF terms). Coverage up to 110% of invoice value.
