Our Credentials

Active Certifications

All certifications below are current and can be shared upon request or included in shipment documentation.

🏆

ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management System

International standard for quality management. Certifies that our processes, documentation, and continuous improvement procedures meet global best practices. Covers production, packing, and export operations.

Quality Management Process Control Continuous Improvement
✓ Valid Renewed: Jan 2024
🔬

HACCP — Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points

Food safety management system that identifies and controls potential hazards in the food supply chain. Required for export to EU, US, Japan, and Australia. Covers all food-grade commodities we export.

Food Safety EU Compliant US FDA Aligned
✓ Valid Renewed: Mar 2024
☪️

Halal Certificate — MUI (Majelis Ulama Indonesia)

Issued by Indonesia's official Islamic council. Certifies all products and processing methods comply with Islamic law. Essential for export to Middle East, Malaysia, GCC, and Muslim-majority markets globally.

Middle East Ready GCC Markets SE Asia
✓ Valid Renewed: Jun 2024
🌿

Phytosanitary Certificate — Ministry of Agriculture RI

Mandatory export document for all plant-based products. Certifies that goods are free from pests, diseases, and non-compliant materials. Issued per shipment by Badan Karantina Pertanian Indonesia.

Per Shipment Plant Products IPPC Compliant
✓ Per Shipment Issued by BKP RI
📋

NIB Ekspor — Export Business License

Nomor Induk Berusaha (Business Identification Number) with export facility, issued by OSS Indonesia. Legally authorizes our company to conduct export activities. Verifiable on OSS.go.id government portal.

Legal Export License Gov. Verified
✓ Active OSS.go.id Verified
🌱

RSPO + ISPO — Sustainable Palm Oil

Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO) and Indonesian Sustainable Palm Oil (ISPO) certification. Required by major EU and US retailers. Covers traceability from plantation to export.

Palm Products Only Sustainability EU Deforestation Reg.
✓ Valid Palm products
